About

Public-service performance should be visible.

CityPulse is building a simple civic accountability layer on top of public 311 records, now starting to compare multiple cities.

Problem

Open data exists. Public accountability is still hard.

Cities publish thousands of 311 records, but raw tables make it hard to see which neighborhoods wait longer, which request types dominate workload, and where data is too thin to judge fairly. CityPulse turns that information into metrics residents can read, compare, and use.

Residents

See neighborhood service quality.

Compare response times and use evidence when asking for faster service.

Advocates

Find evidence-backed service gaps.

Identify neighborhoods or request categories that may need public attention.

Officials

Spot operational pressure points.

Use response patterns to understand where resources may need to shift.
