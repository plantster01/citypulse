About

Public-service data should be understandable.

CityPulse turns public 311 service records into neighborhood-level signals that residents, advocates, journalists, and city leaders can actually use.

Problem

City service records are public, but not always useful.

Boston publishes thousands of 311 records, but raw tables make it hard to see which neighborhoods wait longer, which request types dominate workload, and where data is too thin to judge fairly. CityPulse translates that raw information into clear public metrics.

Residents

Know what is happening nearby.

Compare response times and use evidence when asking for faster service.

Advocates

Find service gaps faster.

Identify neighborhoods or request categories that may need public attention.

Officials

Spot operational pressure.

Use response patterns to understand where resources may need to shift.
