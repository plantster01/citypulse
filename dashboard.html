Data notes

CityPulse keeps the ranking simple and transparent.

The website requests up to 25,000 recently closed public JSON records from each selected 311 source file. For Boston, CityPulse combines the old and new Analyze Boston 311 files before filtering for area names, open and closed dates, and the 30-day outlier guard.
