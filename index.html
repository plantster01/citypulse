How it works

CityPulse converts open civic data into public accountability metrics.

The website requests up to 10,000 public JSON records from the Boston 311 Service Requests API, keeps records that include accurate Boston neighborhood names plus both open and closed dates, calculates resolution hours for each request, then averages those values by neighborhood.

Civic action

Use the numbers as evidence when reporting service gaps.

If your neighborhood shows slower resolution times, cite the average wait and ticket count when contacting city leadership.
