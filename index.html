Data notes

CityPulse keeps the ranking simple and transparent.

The website requests up to 25,000 public JSON records from the selected city’s 311 data source, keeps records with area names plus open and closed dates, uses a 30-day outlier guard for unusually long-running cases, then averages resolution time by area.

Civic action

Use the numbers as evidence when reporting service gaps.

If your area shows slower resolution times, cite the average wait and ticket count when contacting local leadership or filing a follow-up request.
