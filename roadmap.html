Roadmap

From one-city dashboard to civic accountability layer.

CityPulse starts with Boston 311 and can grow into a repeatable way to compare city service quality across neighborhoods and cities.

Now

Boston 311 dashboard

Live rankings, request mix, reliability filtering, and a response-time target simulator.

Next

Neighborhood reports

Auto-generated summaries residents can share with officials or community groups.

Later

Trend detection

Track whether response times are improving or worsening over weeks and months.

Future

Multi-city expansion

Bring the same public accountability metrics to other cities with open 311 data.
